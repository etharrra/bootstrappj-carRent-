Our Vision

Yoma Fleet Limited will be the Myanmar leader in vehicle solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the lowest cost, high quality and most customer focused vehicle hire company in Myanmar, creating value for our shareholders. We will achieve our vision and mission by focusing on and balancing customer satisfaction, employee satisfaction and asset management with equal emphasis on efficiency and growth.

Our Values

  • Passion - Wanting to win now
  • Professionalism - using our skills and expertise effectively and collaborating seamlessly
  • Respect - Valuing the contributions of each other and our partners
  • Responsibility - Being accountable for our decision and actions
  • Innovation - Using imaginations and initiative to drive growth and efficiency
  • Integrity - Acting ethically, fairly, honestly, and transparently
  • Discipline - Applying processes to set and achieve our goals
  • Empowerment - Owning and immediately acting to resolve customer problems
  • Excellence - High quality performance to produce outstanding results & customer for life
  • Execution - Continuously upskilling our people through training
  • Safety - Creating and maintaining an accident-free work environment
  • Presentation - Being proud of our appearance, language and behavior
