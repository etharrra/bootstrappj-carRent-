HOW IT WORK

JOIN

You'll need to get free Tap Card by completely registration in order to access the vehicles.

RESERVE

Log in and select the vehicle you want and how long you need it.

UNLOCK AND DRIVE

Hold your membership card over the windshield sensor to unlock the vehicle. The keys will be waiting for you inside.

RETURN

Return the vehicle at the end of your reservation. Hold your tap card over the windshield sensor. The doors will lock and your rental will end. Access End Trip from your account on our website.
