Why PERFECT7 Car Share?


You just reserve, drive, and return. There’s no cost to sign up, and you’ll never be required to pay a monthly fee. Plus, it’s not only about what you get but also what you can completely forget about like car payments, insurance and parking, just to name a few.

  • Myanmar’s freshest fleet
  • Rented hourly, daily, weekly, monthly
  • One way rental available
  • International standard insurance coverage
  • Available nationwide
  • Reliable

Included in each reservation:

  • Insurance
  • Fuel Inclusive
  • Maintenance
  • Taxes
  • Parking at the station
  • 24/7 help desk
  • Cleaning
  • 24/7 roadside assistance

HOW IT WORK

JOIN

You'll need to get free Tap Card by completely registration in order to access the vehicles.

RESERVE

Log in and select the vehicle you want and how long you need it.

UNLOCK AND DRIVE

Hold your membership card over the windshield sensor to unlock the vehicle. The keys will be waiting for you inside.

RETURN

Return the vehicle at the end of your reservation. Hold your tap card over the windshield sensor. The doors will lock and your rental will end. Access End Trip from your account on our website.
